# House Rules

House Rules help set clear expectations between hosts and guests to ensure a safe, respectful, and comfortable stay for everyone. Guests must review and agree to these rules before booking.

## General Rules

### 1. Check-In & Check-Out
*   **Check-In:** Standard check-in time starts at **3:00 PM** (local time) unless pre-arranged with the host.
*   **Check-Out:** Guests must check out by **11:00 AM** (local time). Late check-outs without approval may result in additional fees.
*   **Key/Access:** Follow the check-in instructions provided by the host. Keep track of keys/fobs; replacements for lost keys will be charged to the guest.

### 2. Guests & Visitors
*   **Registered Guests Only:** Only the guests included in the booking reservation are allowed to stay overnight.
*   **Visitors:** Prior approval from the host is required for any daytime visitors. Unauthorized parties or large gatherings are strictly prohibited.

### 3. Noise and Quiet Hours
*   **Quiet Hours:** Please respect the neighbors. Quiet hours are enforced daily from **10:00 PM to 8:00 AM**.
*   **Noise Levels:** Avoid playing loud music or making excessive noise at any time.

### 4. Smoking and Drugs
*   **Smoke-Free:** Smoking (including cigarettes, e-cigarettes, vapes, and cannabis) is strictly prohibited inside the property.
*   **Fines:** Any violation of the smoking policy will incur a deep cleaning fee.

### 5. Pets
*   **Pet Policy:** Pets are only allowed if explicitly permitted in the listing.
*   **Responsibilities:** Guests are responsible for cleaning up after their pets and any damage caused by them.

### 6. Cleanliness and Care
*   **Trash Disposal:** Dispose of garbage in the designated bins. Recycling should be sorted as per local guidelines.
*   **Kitchen Use:** Clean dishes, utensils, and countertops after use.
*   **Damage Reporting:** Report any accidental damage or breakages to the host as soon as they occur.

---

## Consequences of Rule Violations
Violations of the house rules can lead to:
*   Immediate termination of the reservation without a refund.
*   Additional cleaning or repair fees.
*   Negative reviews on the guest profile.
