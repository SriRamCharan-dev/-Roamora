# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Find answers to common questions about booking, hosting, payments, and resolving issues.

## General Questions

### How do I search and book a listing?
1. Enter your destination, travel dates, and the number of guests in the search bar.
2. Filter search results by price, home type, amenities, and more.
3. Click on a listing to view photos, reviews, house rules, and amenities.
4. Click **Book Now** or **Request to Book** and follow the checkout steps.

### When will I be charged for my booking?
*   If the listing has **Instant Book**, you will be charged immediately upon confirmation.
*   If you send a booking request, the host has 24 hours to accept. You will only be charged once the host accepts the request.

## Guest FAQs

### How do I contact the host?
Before booking, you can click **Contact Host** on the listing details page. Once a booking is confirmed, you can message the host directly through the chat or find their phone number in the trip details.

### What should I do if something is broken or missing upon check-in?
1. Message the host immediately via the chat to inform them of the issue.
2. Give the host a reasonable opportunity to fix or replace the item.
3. If the host is unresponsive or unable to fix a major issue, contact Customer Support within 24 hours with photos or videos to file a claim under our Guest Refund Policy.

### Can I bring more guests than listed on my reservation?
No. The number of guests must match the reservation details. Bringing extra guests without host approval violates the house rules and may result in additional fees or cancellation of your stay.

## Host FAQs

### How do I list my property?
Click on **Become a Host** in the navigation bar. You will be guided through setting up your listing, adding photos, writing descriptions, setting pricing, and defining your house rules.

### How and when do I get paid?
Payouts are typically released to hosts 24 hours after the guest's scheduled check-in time. Depending on your bank and payout method, it may take 1 to 5 business days for the funds to appear in your account.

### What happens if a guest damages my property?
*   **Document the Damage:** Take clear photos or videos of the damage and obtain repair/replacement quotes.
*   **Resolution Center:** Contact the guest through the Resolution Center to request reimbursement.
*   **Support Claim:** If the guest refuses or doesn't respond within 72 hours, escalate the issue to Customer Support to file a claim under our Host Damage Protection policy.
