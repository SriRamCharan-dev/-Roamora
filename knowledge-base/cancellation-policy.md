# Cancellation Policy

We understand that travel plans can change. To provide transparency and fairness to both guests and hosts, we offer standardized cancellation policies. The specific policy for each listing is set by the host and displayed at the time of booking.

## Cancellation Tiers

### 1. Flexible Cancellation Policy
*   **Full Refund:** Guests can cancel up to 24 hours before the local check-in time and receive a full refund (minus service fees, if applicable).
*   **Late Cancellation:** If a guest cancels less than 24 hours before check-in, the first night is non-refundable, but the remaining nights will be refunded at 50%.
*   **Early Departure:** If the guest decides to leave early after check-in, the remaining nights starting 24 hours after the cancellation is submitted are 50% refunded.

### 2. Moderate Cancellation Policy
*   **Full Refund:** Guests must cancel at least 5 days before the local check-in time to receive a full refund.
*   **Partial Refund:** If cancelled within 5 days of check-in, the first night plus 50% of all remaining nights will be refunded.
*   **Early Departure:** If the guest leaves early, the remaining nights starting 24 hours after the cancellation is submitted are 50% refunded.

### 3. Strict Cancellation Policy
*   **Full Refund (Grace Period):** Guests receive a full refund only if they cancel within 48 hours of booking, and at least 14 days before the local check-in time.
*   **50% Refund:** Guests can receive a 50% refund if they cancel at least 7 days before check-in.
*   **No Refund:** Cancellations made within 7 days of check-in are non-refundable.

## Extenuating Circumstances Policy
In rare cases of severe emergencies or natural disasters that make travel impossible or illegal, the standard cancellation policies may be overridden by our **Extenuating Circumstances Policy**. 

Eligible events include:
*   Declared public health emergencies or epidemics.
*   Government-mandated travel restrictions.
*   Natural disasters, severe weather events, or war/hostilities.
*   Military deployment or jury duty.

*Note: Personal illnesses, injuries, or changes in plans do not qualify for extenuating circumstances and fall under the listing's standard cancellation policy.*

## How to Cancel a Reservation
1. Go to your **Trips** or **Bookings** page.
2. Select the reservation you wish to cancel.
3. Click on **Cancel Reservation**.
4. Review the refund breakdown based on the listing's policy before confirming.
5. Complete the steps to finalize the cancellation.
