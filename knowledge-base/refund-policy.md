# Guest Refund Policy

Our Guest Refund Policy protects guests against major travel issues or inaccuracies in listings. If a guest encounters an eligible issue, we will work to find a comparable alternative listing or issue a partial or full refund.

## Eligible Travel Issues

A "Travel Issue" refers to any of the following situations:

1.  **Host Misrepresentation:** 
    *   The host cancels the reservation shortly before check-in or fails to provide access (e.g., wrong lockbox code, host is unreachable).
    *   The listing description is significantly inaccurate regarding size, number of rooms, location, or key amenities (e.g., listing advertised a pool or hot tub, but none exists or they are completely broken).
2.  **Safety and Cleanliness Issues:**
    *   The listing is not clean, sanitary, or safe (e.g., dirty linens, mold, pest infestation).
    *   The accommodation contains safety or health hazards that were not disclosed.
3.  **Lack of Essential Amenities:**
    *   Major missing items that were advertised as included (e.g., heating, air conditioning, electricity, running water, or working bathrooms).

---

## How to Submit a Refund Claim

To be eligible for a refund, guests must follow these steps:

1.  **Contact the Host First:** You must notify your host about the issue immediately to give them a chance to resolve it.
2.  **Document the Issue:** Take clear photos or videos of the problem (e.g., cleanliness issues, broken amenities, lack of hot water).
3.  **Submit the Claim Within 24 Hours:** Contact our support team within 24 hours of discovering the travel issue. Provide your reservation details, description of the problem, and visual evidence.
4.  **Do Not Complete the Stay:** If you intend to request a full refund, you must leave the listing and not complete your stay. Remaining in the property may limit your eligibility to a partial refund.

---

## Refund Calculations

*   **Full Refunds:** Typically granted if the issue prevents the guest from occupying the listing entirely (e.g., host cancellation, lock-out, or severe safety issues upon check-in) and the guest leaves.
*   **Partial Refunds:** If the issue is minor or resolved during the stay, or if the guest decides to stay despite the issue, a partial refund representing the diminished value of the stay will be calculated.
